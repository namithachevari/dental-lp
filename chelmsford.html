When Do You Need Oral Surgery?

You may need oral surgery for common procedures such as wisdom tooth extractions, dental implants, impacted or multiple teeth removal, periodontal disease, other cosmetic or reconstructive dentistry, reconstructive TMJ Disorders, facial injuries, or fractures. Many procedures are performed with sedation dentistry or laser dentistry and patients can resume normal activities in just a few days.

Dental Implants

Dental implants are a good choice when replacing missing teeth and restoring a beautiful smile. Dental implants are artificial roots and teeth that are surgically placed into the upper or lower jawbone by a dentist, oral surgeon, or periodontist. The “teeth” or crowns attached to implants are natural-looking and effectively enhance or restore your smile. Implants can also provide a fixed solution to removable partial or complete dentures.
